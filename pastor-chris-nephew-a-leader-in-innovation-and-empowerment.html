Pastor Chris' Nephew: A leader in innovation and empowerment

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Early Life and Influences of Pastor Chris’ Nephew

Born into a family deeply rooted in faith, Daysman Woghiren Oyakhilome was nurtured in an environment that emphasized compassion, integrity, and a strong connection with God.

His mother, Pastor Chris’ sister, Kathy Woghiren, serves as the Director of the LoveWorld Creative Arts Academy and the LoveWorld Music and Arts Ministry, further embedding him in the foundation and ethos of the ministry from a young age.

A Commitment to Education and Faith

Understanding the importance of intellectual growth alongside spiritual development, Daysman pursued higher education, combining academic achievements with his deepening spiritual awareness. This balance became a hallmark of his character, setting the stage for his future endeavours in service and enlightenment.

Most recently, in May 2024, Daysman Woghiren Oyakhilome, the nephew of Pastor Chris, achieved a significant milestone by earning a Doctor of Philosophy Degree in Business Administration and Humanities from ISMT St Salomon University in Cotonou, Benin Republic. This prestigious accomplishment showcases his dedication and hard work.

Daysman shared his excitement and gratitude, saying, "This achievement, made possible by God, will enable me to take the world for God." This degree isn't just a personal triumph; it also highlights the importance of education within the Christian community, serving as an inspiration for many in Christ Embassy and beyond.

With this advanced knowledge in business administration and humanities, Daysman is now even better equipped to contribute to his community and ministry. His education provides him with the tools to address modern challenges creatively and effectively, enhancing his role in both spiritual and secular spheres.

As he continues to integrate his academic expertise with his ministry work, Daysman's impact on the community is expected to grow. His commitment to using his education for the greater good underscores the vital role that knowledge plays in spiritual and community development.

Leadership in Ministry and Media

Daysman's journey in the ministry has been characterized by humility and a genuine desire to make a positive impact. As a preacher, he resonates with audiences worldwide, delivering sermons that inspire and uplift. His reach extends beyond traditional congregations through modern technology and online platforms, making his messages accessible globally.

In addition to his preaching, Daysman has made significant strides in faith-based media. As the CEO of Carel Films Enterprise, he has produced over 100 Christian music videos, including notable works like "I Know Who I Am" by Sinach and "Kinging" by Rozey. His innovative approach to storytelling and visual communication has garnered him multiple awards, including the American Academy of Animations Award and the Walt Disney Award​​.

Empowering the Next Generation

Daysman’s vision extends to empowering future leaders through education and mentorship. As a key figure in the LoveWorld Next Academy and the LoveWorld Institute of Innovation and Technology, he plays a vital role in nurturing young talents. His initiatives aim to equip the next generation with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in ministry and beyond.

Through strategic partnerships and innovative solutions, Daysman ensures that quality education and technological advancements are accessible, fostering creativity and excellence in the LoveWorld community and beyond​.

Pastor Chris’ Nephew: A Heart for Service

A core principle of Daysman’s ministry involves a commitment to serving the less fortunate. Inspired by teachings of compassion, he spearheads various charitable initiatives addressing issues such as poverty, education, and healthcare. These efforts reflect his dedication to uplifting communities and exemplify the transformative power of faith in action​.

Overcoming Challenges

Daysman's journey has not been without challenges. However, his ability to navigate adversities with unwavering faith and resilience has become a source of inspiration. His close relationship with Pastor Chris, marked by mutual respect and support, has played a crucial role in his growth and success. Daysman acknowledges the importance of guidance and correction from his uncle, viewing it as a blessing that has shaped his character and future​.

Pastor Chris’ Nephew Shines in His Own Light

Looking ahead, Daysman envisions expanding the LoveWorld Next Academy and leveraging faith-based media to inspire positive change. His commitment to innovation and education aims to address emerging trends and equip students with cutting-edge skills. By embracing modern technology and strategic partnerships, Daysman is poised to extend his impact globally, making quality education and inspirational content accessible to all.

Pastor Chris' nephew, Daysman Woghiren Oyakhilome, embodies the Oyakhilome family legacy of faith, service, and innovation. His multifaceted contributions to ministry, media, and education highlight a commitment to excellence and a vision for a brighter, more hopeful future. Through his work, Daysman continues to inspire and uplift, making a lasting impact on communities around the world.
