Kogi State University cut-off mark, courses, admission and school fees (2024/2025)

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Kogi State University (KSU) (now Prince Abubakar Audu University) is state-owned in Anyigba, Kogi, Nigeria. It was established in 1999 by Prince Abubakar Audu, the state's former governor. It has been ranked as Nigeria's fastest-developing university and one of the most popular universities in Africa. The Kogi State University cut-off mark for 2024/2025 will help you understand whether you can secure a place there as a student prospect.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Kogi State University/Prince Abubakar Audu University (PAAU) is known for its thorough admission process, admitting students strictly by merit. It is among the best universities in Nigeria, with some of its departments ranked by Nigerian professional and academic bodies as the best in the country.

Kogi State University cut-off mark

For the 2024/2025 academic session, Kogi State University (KSU) has set its JAMB cut-off mark at 140. This means students who scored 140 or above in the UTME can apply for admission into KSU.

When will Kogi State University start giving admission?

The Kogi State University (KSU) management confirmed that the admission process for the 2024/2025 academic session will begin on 15 August 2024.

KSU admission requirements

Apart from meeting the KSU cut-off mark, you must fulfil other eligibility criteria. Below are the requirements.

  • Candidates must have sat for the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME).
  • To be eligible for the post-UTME examination and admission screening exercise, candidates must meet the KSU cut-off mark and the courses offered.
  • Candidates must possess at least 5 credit passes in relevant subjects, including English Language and Mathematics at GCE O’level, WASSCE, SSCE, NECO or approved equivalents at not more than two (2) Sittings.
  • Applicants must be at least sixteen (16) years of age in the admission year.
  • Satisfy KSU department and faculty requirements before being admitted.

How can I check my admission to KSU?

To check your 2024/2025 admission list for undergraduate candidates, students must log in to the student portal using their UTME username and password. To check, follow these steps:

  • Go to the KSU admission checking portal through www.portal.jamb.gov.ng/efacility.
  • Enable the Desktop Site option in your Google Chrome browser settings.
  • Log in with your JAMB email and password.
  • Scroll down and click on the “Check Admission Status” tab or CAPS.
  • Choose your exam year if prompted.
  • Enter your JAMB registration number if required.
  • Click on “Check Admission Status” to view your KSU admission status.

    How much is Kogi State University school fees?

    The updated school fees of Kogi State University, Anyigba, for indigenous students, range between N80,000 and N100,000, depending on the program, and N180,050 to N200,050 for non-indigenous students.

    How much is Kogi State University school fees for freshers? For new students, the school fees range from ₦57,500 to ₦100,000 and ₦46,000 to ₦89,000 for returning students. If you are a new or returning student, you must check the department’s fees on the school’s official website.
