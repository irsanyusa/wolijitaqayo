24 Split Tattoos That Are Pure Poetry for the Eyes

Publish date: 2024-06-30

A tattoo can be more than just a decorative pattern on your body. Some cultures even consider them sacred, and it’s no wonder — they’re a visual representation of what goes on in your head, and in a way, they can define you. Some tattoo artists embrace this and have managed to make true masterpieces embedded in their clients’ skin.

But as if that wasn’t enough, they even manage to use the body in an organic way to create visual effects. These tattoos are called split tattoos or interlocking tattoos. Bright Side wants to inspire you with a compilation of these creative masterpieces. Who knows, maybe you’ll end up getting inked soon too.
