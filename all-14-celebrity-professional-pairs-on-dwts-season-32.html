The full cast of Dancing with the Stars Season 32 was officially announced on Wednesday, in addition to which celebrities will be dancing with which pro dancers. Here are the pairs for this season of Dancing With the Stars.

Season 32 will premiere on Tuesday, September 26 simultaneously on ABC and Disney+. This season, the show will premiere on Tuesdays. Judges Bruno Tonioli, Carrie Ann Inaba, and Derek Hough will return to the ballroom this season. Alfonso Ribeiro will also return as co-host, beside his new co-host Julianne Hough.

Dancing With The Stars Season 32 Pairs

1. Peta Murgatroyd, Barry Williams

DWTS veteran Peta Murgatroyd is back again! This season, she’s paired with Brady Bunch star Barry Williams.

2. Emma Slater, Mauricio Umansky

Emma Slater is paired with Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and Buying Beverly Hills star Mauricio Umansky.

3. Rylee Arnold, Harry Jowsey

DWTS first timer Rylee Arnold is paired with Too Hot To Handle star Harry Jowsey.

4. Artem Chigvintsev, Charity Lawson

Professional dancer Artem Chigvintsev is paired with the Bachelorette Charity Lawson.

5. Sasha Farber, Alyson Hannigan

Longtime pro Sasha Farber is paired with How I Met Your Mother star Alyson Hannigan.

6. Gleb Savchenko, Mira Sorvino

Fan favorite DWTS pro Gleb Savchenko is paired with Academy Award winner Mira Sorvino.

7. Daniella Karagach, Jason Mraz

New mother Daniella Karagach returns to the ballroom this season with music icon Jason Mraz.

8. Britt Stewart, Adrian Peterson

Britt Stewart is paired with NFL running back Adrian Peterson this season.

9. Alan Bersten, Jamie Lynn Spears

Fan favorite pro Alan Bersten is paired with Zoey 101 star Jamie Lynn Spears.

10. Jenna Johnson, Tyson Beckford

New mother Jenna Johnson is paired with fashion icon Tyson Beckford this season.

11. Brandon Armstrong, Lele Pons

This season, Brandon Armstrong is waltzing into the ballroom with Vine, YouTube, and TikTok star Lele Pons.

12. KoKo Iwasaki, Matt Walsh

KoKo Iwasaki will compete with Veep star Matt Walsh this season.

13. Val Chmerkovskiy, Xochitl Gomez

Val Chmerkovskiy is taking the ballroom with Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness star Xochitl Gomez.

14. Pasha Pashkov, Ariana Madix

Pasha Pashkov is paired with Vanderpump Rules star Ariana Madix.
