Style Guide: How to dress like a Tech Bro in 5 steps

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Do you want to dress like a Tech Bro or recognise their outfits? Here’s how;

Tech bros may be UI/UX designers, front-end developers, back-end developers, full stack developers, data analysts and a whole bunch of other related activities.

But, what do Tech bros wear?

1. Sweatpants/Joggers

ADVERTISEMENT

With Tech bros, sweatpants are sine qua non. Don’t be surprised if you see them wearing them to work, dinner, picnics, everywhere!

2. Hoodies

A very close relative of sweatpants are hoodies. Tech bros wear hoodies with sweatpants and shorts.

ADVERTISEMENT

Hoodies are so pivotal to tech bros wardrobe that they are given as a welcome present when they join tech companies.

3. Dreadlocks or dishevelled hair

Whether it is dreadlocked or not, most tech bros, especially males, hardly have time to use a comb and end up with dishevelled afro.

4. Eyeglasses

ADVERTISEMENT

Hours of staring at a computer screen can either damage their eyes or they may take a preemptive measure and start wearing blue-light glasses.

5. Crocs/slides and socks or sneakers and socks

On a normal day, you are most likely to catch a tech bro wearing crocs and long, nerdy colorful socks or sneakers, mostly Nikes and socks.

That's it for us, but we are you were picturing a Tech Bro as you read this article.
