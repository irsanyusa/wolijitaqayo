Samsung Galaxy A10e specs, review, price, details

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Samsung Galaxy A10e is a low budget phone released in August. It has enough storage space as it comes with 32GB ROM and 2GB RAM. It runs on the Android 9.0 (Pie) version. Its overall display and performance are quite admirable.

Unlike most recent phones in the market, the device does not have a fingerprint scanner. It was made to target individuals who go for affordability more than anything else.

Overview

Here is a brief overview of the pros and cons of the device to help you decide if it is a good choice to go for.

The good

  • It has a beautiful display with a drop notch design
  • It has the facial recognition unlock feature that responds quite fast
  • Relatively enough internal memory for all your documents, images, and applications
  • Compatible with all Samsung devices and wearables
  • It is budget-friendly

The bad

  • It does not have a fingerprint scanner
  • Does not support wireless charging
  • Pictures taken under low light have shallow detail
  • The plastic back is glossy and smudges easily

Hardware

The device has an elegant design which you will love. Take a look at the device's other features:

Design

The device has a plastic body, and the screen is made of glass. It has a thickness of 8.4 mm and weighs 141g, making it easy to carry around. Samsung Galaxy A10e colors are limited to only one; black.

On the bottom of the device, you have a 3.5 mm headphone jack, Type-C charging port and speaker. On the left side is the tray that houses the SIM card and which you can put a microSD for extra storage. The noise cancellation mic is placed on the top. The main camera and Samsung logo are placed on the glossy back cover.

Samsung Galaxy A10e display

The phone has a 5.83-inch display with a waterdrop notch at the top. It has a PLS TFT capacitive touchscreen with a color depth of 16M. It has an impressive 81.4% screen to body ratio. The screen resolution is 720 x 1560 pixels, with an aspect ratio of 19.5:9. The 295 ppi density is, however, quite low compared to most recent phones which have more than 500 ppi.

Processor, storage, and performance

The device is powered by an Exynos 7884 (14nm) chipset and an octa-core processor. The Mali-G71 MP2 graphics processor will boost your gaming and overall phone usage experience. The response speed is fast enough, and the device rarely experiences lagging.

The phone has enough storage and can be expanded up to 512 GB. It offers 32GB of ROM and 2GB of RAM.

Battery

This phone comes with a non-removable 3000 mAh Li-Ion battery. The Samsung Galaxy A10e battery life is decent depending on your usage, network, signal strength, and accessories used. The device supports Adaptive Fast Charging and might get warm when charging. However, it does not come with the wireless charging feature.

Speaker and call quality

The speakers are loud enough and with crisp sound quality when watching videos or simply listening to music. It also comes with a 3.5 mm headphone jack. The noise cancellation mic and earpiece ensure your calls are clear and of good quality.

Software

Here are the software features that accompany the device:

OS and features

The phone runs on the Android 9.0 (Pie) version. Samsung Galaxy A10e Android 10 upgrade will be expected next year. For a low budget device, the upgrade is a plus. It will feature smaller notification pop-ups, enhanced dark modes, and simplified button arrangements.

Even though it does not have a fingerprint sensor, it has accelerometer and proximity sensors.

Camera, video, and image quality

The device has a single primary camera that can be either 8 MP, f/1.9 or 5 MP. The primary camera features an LED flash, panorama, and HDR for photo enhancement. It has an option of digital zoom.

The images have good colour saturation, especially during the day. In low light, the colours get a little bit washed out and detail is lost. For portrait pictures, the background is blurred perfectly to give you a decent picture. The 5 MP selfie images have good detail and can also be enhanced with the beauty feature. The video quality is also impressive with a resolution of 1080p@30fps.

Verdict

For a low budget device, the phone has enough storage and great performance. The image quality is also decent. Samsung Galaxy A10e price ranges between N40,000 and N49,000 depending on where you purchase the phone.

The Samsung Galaxy A10e is a good device for everyday use. It is available in both online and offline stores in Nigeria.
