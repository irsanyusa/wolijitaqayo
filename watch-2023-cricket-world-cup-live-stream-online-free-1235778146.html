It all comes down to this: India faces Australia on Nov. 19 at 3:30 a.m. ET / 12:30 a.m. PT in the finals of the 2023 Cricket World Cup, one of the premiere sporting events internationally.

Officially known as the ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup, the event pits ten of the best cricket nations in the world against each other for a chance to hoist the official trophy of the One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament.

How to Watch 2023 Cricket World Cup Online

The 2023 ICC Cricket World Cup kicked off Oct. 5 and runs through Nov. 19. India, South Africa, Australia and New Zealand all qualified for the semifinals at the end of round-robin play, with India defeating South Africa and Australia taking down the Kiwis to qualify for the final.

2023 Cricket World Cup Odds, Predictions

This is the 13th edition of the One Day International cricket tournament, which is a quadrennial event. The Cricket World Cup is also one of the world’s most-viewed sporting events, with more than one billion people expected to tune in for this year’s edition.

England are the current champions after winning the 2019 World Cup edition. Australia is the most successful Cricket World Cup nation of all time, with five titles to their name.

Still, India is the odds-on favorites for this year’s trophy, with bookmakers citing their impressive roster of stars and the host nation’s advantage. In fact, India kicked off their 2023 Cricket World Cup campaign with six straight wins. Need more proof that India is a favorite: the last three World Cups were won by the home team.
