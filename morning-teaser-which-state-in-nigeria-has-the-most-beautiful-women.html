Which state in Nigeria has the most beautiful women?

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Some will say women of the Fulani stock take the lead in the beauty department while the Hausas will lay claim as having the most stunning beauties.

The Efiks/Ibibios from Cross River and Akwa Ibom States stand out but the Igbos, Binis, Yorubas, Ijaws, Urhobos and the Idomas will proudly beat their chests to say they can stand up to be counted.

This piece is not to disparage any Nigerian tribe but just to have a healthy and fun-filled, no-holds-barred beauty contest.
