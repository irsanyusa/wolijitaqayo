Dancing with the Stars and The Masked Singer alum Wayne Brady was reportedly involved in a car accident over the weekend. TMZ reports that Brady got into a physical altercation with the driver of the other car.

Wayne Brady Gets into Fight After Car Accident

TMZ broke the news on Monday that Brady reportedly got into a car accident in Malibu on Sunday night while driving on the Pacific Coast Highway. According to the outlet, another car hit Brady.

He and the other driver (a 51-year-old man) reportedly pulled over and got into an argument that turned physical. According to police, Brady called 911 after the fight. The other driver reportedly got back into his car and drove away.

TMZ reports that police eventually found the other driver and arrested him for battery, DUI, and hit-and-run. The fire department also apparently responded, but Brady didn’t need to be taken to the hospital. Sources told TMZ that he’s “banged up and sore.”

Brady has not commented publicly on the incident. As of this writing, his last post on Instagram is from November 10.

Brady Appeared on The Masked Singer, DWTS

Brady, who is best known for his comedy work on Whose Line Is It Anyway?, has a history with talent shows. He appeared as Fox on the second season of The Masked Singer, and was named the winner of the season.

Then last year, Brady competed on Season 31 of Dancing with the Stars with professional partner Witney Carson. Brady made it all the way to the Finals, where he finished the competition in third place behind winner Charli D’Amelio and runner-up Gabby Windey.

Brady has also performed on Broadway, appearing in productions of Chicago and Kinky Boots. Next year, he’ll star as the title character in a Broadway revival of The Wiz.
