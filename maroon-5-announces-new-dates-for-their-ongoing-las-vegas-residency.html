Maroon 5 Announces New Dates For Their Ongoing Las Vegas Residency

Publish date: 2024-07-20

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment

Photo by Astrid Stawiarz/Getty Images for Audacy

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Iconic pop rock band Maroon 5 has announced 16 new tour dates in 2024 for the continuation of their highly successful Las Vegas residency show, M5LV The Residency. The show will return to the Dolby Live theater at Park MGM in May, June, September, and October of next year after a strong run of concerts in 2023.

Maroon 5 Adds More Dates to Their Blockbuster Vegas Residency

After blowing away fans and critics with the first leg of their intimate Las Vegas residency this year, Maroon 5 is headed back to Sin City in 2024 for an additional 16 dates from May to October. The beloved pop rockers made the announcement this week, revealing four separate blocks of multi-night stands at the spectacular Dolby Live theater located inside the Park MGM casino and hotel.

According to a statement from the band, the visually stunning residency show was custom-built for the unique Dolby Live venue and “every aspect of the show’s immersive design caters to the theater.” Cutting-edge production elements like panoramic video screens and a state-of-the-art Dolby Atmos surround sound system ensure that all 5,200 seats in the house get an electrifying experience.

Fans can expect to hear all of Maroon 5’s huge hits and fan favorites from across the band’s 20-year career blasted through the high-tech sound system as dazzling visuals engulf the intimate theater.

The 2024 leg of the residency will kick off with six shows from May 17 to 31 before a single show on June 1. After a summer break, Maroon 5 returns in late September for two more nights then wraps up the year with a six-night stand in early October. In total, the new dates span four separate runs and 16 nights, certainly giving fans plenty of opportunities to catch one of music’s best live bands in the prime concert destination of Las Vegas.

Adam Levine Calls Career, Family Life “A Gift”

Fresh off a huge year that included the launch of the band’s Las Vegas residency, as well as the birth of his third child, Maroon 5 frontman Adam Levine has a positive outlook on both his professional success and family life. In an interview, the singer opened up about his enthusiasm for returning to Vegas in 2024 and the joy he feels being a husband and father.

Levine gushed about his personal life. Calling his family and relationship with his wife Behati Prinsloo a “gift,” the star professed that his home life actually means more to him than even his wildly successful music career.

“I don’t know how you can have a better life.” Levine said. “Having both music and family is just a gift, and I think you don’t appreciate sh*t like that until you get a little older.”

As he and Maroon 5 balance working on new music and prepping for Vegas, Levine remains cognizant of how rare his situation is. While riding high professionally, Levine is still focused on his role as a husband and father as well.
