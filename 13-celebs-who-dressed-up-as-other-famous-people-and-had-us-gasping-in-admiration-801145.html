13 Celebs Who Dressed Up as Other Famous People and Had Us Gasping in Admiration

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Each year at Halloween time, Hollywood celebrities have the world’s eyes on them to see what their costumes will be. They have the ability to hire professional makeup artists and designers just for the occasion. Therefore, they create looks that are almost identical to other famous people’s iconic outfits. From fake hair, beards, and body implants to expensive clothing, these stars manage to turn their Halloween costumes into red carpet-worthy getups.

Bright Side would like to share 13 of the most spectacular times when celebrities showed their love for other famous people by copying their outfits.

1. Kim Kardashian and Jonathan Cheban as Sonny and Cher

2. John Legend and Chrissy Teigen as Queen Elizabeth and Prince Philip

3. Kourtney Kardashian as Ariana Grande

4. Nina Dobrev as Billie Eilish

5. Ashley Graham as Jessica Rabbit

6. Neil Patrick Harris and David Burtka as Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen.

7. Normani as Naomi Campbell

8. Jesse Tyler Ferguson and Justin Mikita as Billie Eilish and Finneas.

9. Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner as Morticia and Gomez Addams

10. Ciara as Princess Nakia from Black Panther

11. Kevin Hart as Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson

12. Kelly Clarkson and her team as the cast from Hocus Pocus

13. Kris Jenner as Elizabeth Taylor in Cleopatra

Have you ever dressed up as a famous celebrity? If so, which iconic outfit did you choose to copy?
