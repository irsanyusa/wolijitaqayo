Snoop Dogg Launches Dr. Bombay Ice Cream Line

Publish date: 2024-09-06

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Axelle/Bauer-Griffin/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Snoop Dogg has just entered another business venture with the launch of Dr. Bombay ice cream line. Named after the rapper’s Bored Ape NFT, the new brand offers seven luscious scooping flavors, now available at select Walmart stores.

Snoop Dogg Transforms His NFT Into a Delectable Ice Cream Line

For this new commercial endeavor, Snoop Dogg has teamed up with Happi Co., a consumer packaged goods company. The rapper’s NFT character turned sidekick Dr. Bombay serves as the “name, face, and persona” of the line. 

“Ice cream is more than just a snack to me; it’s a way to chill, relax, and get happy,” he said. “That’s exactly what I want Dr. Bombay Ice Cream to do—bring a smile to your face and ease your mind. I’ve poured my heart and soul into perfecting these first seven flavors. I can’t wait for my fans and the world to experience what I’ve created.”

Snoop Dogg’s ice cream line offers seven delectable flavors: Bonus Track Brownie, Cocoa Cream Cookie Dream, Iced Out Orange Cream, Rollin’ In the Dough, S’more Vibes, Syrupy Waffle Sundaze, and Tropical Sherbet Swizzle. 

“Dr. Bombay is here to take you on a fun and funky flavor journey like no other,” the line’s official website reads. “Dr. Bombay Ice Cream is a result of Snoop Dogg’s innovative view on creating unique flavors and an experience that stays with you.”

With the ice cream line, Dr. Bombay promises to serve a “creamy, dreamy, mind-freezing sensation” in every spoonful. Furthermore, it aims to provide consumers a 90’s hip hop and laid back vibe.

Dr. Bombay Ice Cream Line Have Been Receiving Mixed Reviews

Since its release on July 24, some consumers have already tried the product. Dr. Bombay ice cream has so far been getting mixed reviews from the public. Apparently, most who have tried the line liked the Syrupy Waffle Sundaze the most.

“The waffle and s’mores are genuinely the most worthwhile,” one user commented in Snackgator’s Instagram post. 

“I have the waffle one, can confirm it is REALLY good,” one person wrote.

Moreover, some users didn’t think that the Cocoa Cream Cookie Dream was good. On the other hand, in an Instagram post by Snackolator, Iced Out Orange Cream seems to be some users’ best pick. 

“Got the orange one, really good. It’s like the icy part of a popsicle combined with orange sherbet and vanilla. Rollin in the Dough is okay, just a vanilla base kinda disappointed, the mixins are sparse,” one user commented.

Snoop Dogg’s Dr. Bombay ice cream line is already available in 3,500 Walmart stores and sold for $4.98.
