What does it mean if you pierce your left nose?

If you got your nose pierced, it means you have a quick and bold personality, but it also means several things.

1. Piercing your left nose brings good luck

In ancient Chinese culture, piercing your left nostril drives away negative energy and keeps you happy and in high spirits.
