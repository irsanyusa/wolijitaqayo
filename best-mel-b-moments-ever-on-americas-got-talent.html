Best Mel B Moments Ever On 'America's Got Talent'

As fans eagerly await the return of Mel B, on America’s Got Talent’s spin off, AGT Fantasy League, it’s the perfect time to take a stroll down memory lane. Let’s look back at and celebrate the top 10 unforgettable Mel B moments.

Best Mel B Moments Ever On ‘America’s Got Talent’

“Oh My God” Moments – AGT 2017

In 2017, Mel B became the queen of one-liners with her signature catchphrase, “Oh my God!” Whether she was stunned by mind-blowing performances or left in fits of laughter, Mel B’s spontaneous and infectious reactions became a highlight of the season.

Mel B’s Hilarious Performance With Tape Face

During the AGT 2016 finale, Tape Face, a mime, pulled off a comical prank that had everyone in stitches. He strategically placed a toilet seat on Mel B’s head, creating one of the season’s most memorable and side-splitting moments.

Mel B Stands Up to Simon Cowell

During the 2017 audition round, Simon Cowell playfully teased Mel B about her dancing skills, prompting her to respond with another well-aimed cup of water. Other than that, their on-screen banter and playful fights offer great entertainment for the show.

Mel B’s Hair-Raising Moment with Sacred Riana

In a spine-tingling performance, horror magician Sacred Riana left Mel B absolutely terrified. Riana’s eerie act involved a baby doll, an imaginary friend, and a handwritten note in a blood-like substance. The chilling encounter had Mel B seeking refuge and declaring, “This is too much for me,” as she ran offstage, leaving the audience and judges in bewilderment.

Mel B Does Stand-Up Comedy

During the AGT 2018 finale, Mel B surprised everyone by making her stand-up comedy debut while dressed in a unicorn onesie. Cowell and the audience couldn’t hide their embarrassment as Mel B took the stage in her PJs, attempted a few jokes from her giant joke book, and eventually delivered a punchline about a mushroom’s friends, leaving everyone in stitches and eager for her to return to her musical roots.

Mel B’s Spit Fight With Howie Mandel

During the AGT Season 13 quarterfinal, things took an unexpected turn when Howie Mandel turned to Mel B and spit water at her following Human Fountains’ performance. In a surprising twist, Mel B retaliated by spitting water back at him. This created a hilarious and slightly uncomfortable moment as Mandel grappled with his germophobia.

Mel B Almost Kissed Heidi Klum

During magician Collins Key’s mesmerizing performance on AGT, he enlisted the help of Mel B and Heidi Klum for a close-up card trick. The act took an unexpected turn when Mel B and Klum nearly kissed as part of the illusion, leaving the judges and audience in amazement and laughter.

Mel B Stabs Simon Cowell

Mel B playfully “stabbed” Cowell as a form of revenge for his cheeky comment about her marriage. During a mind trick routine performed by Colin Cloud, inspired by Sherlock Holmes, Mel B enthusiastically grabbed a rubber knife and pretended to plunge it into Cowell’s chest from behind

Mel B Vs Heidi Klum Glam Off

In a humorous moment, Mel B and Klum engaged in a mock “glam-off” as they prepared to go on stage. The two judges playfully competed to outdo each other in terms of glamour, donning bold jewelry, thick makeup, and also outlandish gowns in a bid to be the most glamorous.

Mel B Gives Darci Lynne Her Golden Buzzer

One of the most heartwarming moments in AGT history was when Mel B hit the Golden Buzzer for the incredibly talented ventriloquist Darci Lynne. As Darci Lynne took the stage with her puppet, Petunia, she captivated both the audience and the judges with her remarkable performance. Mel B, deeply moved by the young contestant’s talent, couldn’t contain her excitement and pressed the Golden Buzzer, sending Darci Lynne straight to the live shows and making her dream come true.

Magician Makes Mel B’s Diamond Ring Disappear

Magician Kevin Blake stunned Mel B during the Judge Cuts round by making her diamond ring disappear into the sky using a bunch of balloons. He later magically revealed the ring, leaving everyone amazed.

Mel B Is Always One Step Behind

Cowell and Mandel engaged in some playful banter on AGT when discussing Mel B’s live show delay. They joked about how she often needed explanations and had a delayed reaction to jokes, sparking laughter from the audience.
