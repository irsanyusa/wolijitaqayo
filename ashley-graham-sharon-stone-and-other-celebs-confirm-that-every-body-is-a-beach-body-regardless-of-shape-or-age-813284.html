Ashley Graham, Sharon Stone, and Other Celebs Confirm That Every Body Is a Beach Body Regardle

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Any body can be considered a beach or a bikini body. It’s not about conforming to society’s narrow standards of beauty or having a specific physique. It’s about embracing and celebrating your body, no matter its shape, size, or imperfections. Whether you’re curvy, athletic, petite, or anything in between and no matter how old you are, you have every right to feel confident and beautiful in a bikini just like our celebs.

Ashley Graham

Ashley Graham is a true icon of body positivity and acceptance. The plus-size model constantly shares pictures of herself in bikinis on Instagram where she doesn’t shy away from showing her stretch marks and cellulite in the unretouched snaps.

Demi Moore

Demi Moore is an advocate for feeling and looking your best at any age. The 60-year-old actress enjoys sporting swimsuits and wearing string bikinis while chilling at the pool. Moore doesn’t hesitate to pose in bikinis alongside her young daughters, and they all look equally stunning.

Lizzo

Lizzo also doesn’t hesitate to publicly show off in a bikini. The singer always gets support from her fans for showing her curvaceous figure calling her “the definition of real.” In the video of her chilling in a pool, she declares, “I am absolutely stunning. I am the epitome of beauty standards. So, catch up, my dear!”

Sam Smith

Sam Smith exudes body confidence by rocking a small pair of animal print bikini bottoms. The non-binary singer, age 30, appeared to be enjoying a blissful vacation in December, basking in the winter sun on a yacht.

Brooke Shields

While enjoying her vacation Brooke Shields, the 57-year-old model and actress, has been delighting her audience by sharing remarkable photos from her fantastic beach getaway. In one of her social media posts, she treated her followers to a mirror selfie showcasing her impeccably sculpted midsection while wearing an adorable floral 2-piece swimsuit.

Barbie Ferreira

Euphoria star, Barbie Ferreira, consistently shares breathtaking photos of her outfits on her social media account. And she has certainly set a new standard for celebrity beach pictures with her Instagram snapshots where she proudly displays her bikini body.

Britney Spears

Britney Spears, the 41-year-old pop sensation, confidently flaunts her well-toned physique in a variety of stylish swimwear. The singer constantly showcases her bikini looks on Instagram and looks gorgeous no matter what age she is.

Sharon Stone

Sharon Stone, at the age of 65, proves that being 60 can still be incredibly sexy. And one of her Instagram posts, where she poses in a bikini, proves that. She serves as an inspiration, proving that beauty and allure know no boundaries when it comes to age.

Amy Schumer

On National Bikini Day, numerous celebrities take to social media to showcase their toned abs. Amy Schumer joined in by sharing a confident photo that received a lot of positive attention where she was lying in a relaxed pose and enjoying the sun. The post received praise from her followers and fellow celebrities like Lena Dunham and Demi Lovato who commented, “This is everything.”

Catherine Zeta-Jones

Catherine Zeta-Jones created quite the buzz with her Instagram post when she showed herself in a bikini. The 53-year-old actress shared a video of herself hula-hooping while enjoying a summer vacation and got many warm comments from fans.

Although it’s essential to accept and love yourself at any age, it’s also important to have moderate physical activities in your daily life. Exercise may help you stay fit and keep your mind sharp.
