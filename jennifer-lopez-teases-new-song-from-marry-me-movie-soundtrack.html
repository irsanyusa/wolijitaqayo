Former World of Dance judge Jennifer Lopez has a new romantic comedy coming out in February called Marry Me, co-starring Owen Wilson. She’s already released one song from the movie’s soundtrack, and now she’s teasing another one, called “After Love.”

Jennifer Lopez Teases New Song ‘After Love’

Lopez previously released the song “On My Way” from the Marry Me soundtrack, along with a glamorous music video. The movie will also feature the track “After Love.” Lopez posted an Instagram video previewing the song, featuring clips from the film.

“What comes after love? / I wanna know there’s something for me after us,” Lopez sings on the track. It looks like her character will be performing the song in the movie, at a school dance she attends with Owen Wilson’s character.

The premise of the movie is that Lopez plays a pop star who finds out her fiancé is cheating on her. She decides to marry a concertgoer who accidentally holds a “Marry Me” sign in the crowd. They end up getting married on stage. The film is out in theaters February 11, and streaming on Peacock the same day.

When Will the ‘Marry Me’ Soundtrack Be Available?

The official Marry Me soundtrack will be released February 4, one week before the movie debuts. The album features 13 tracks, and Lopez performs most of the songs. Colombian singer Maluma, who plays her fiancé in the film, is also featured on several songs.

In addition to “On My Way” and “After Love,” the soundtrack also features the songs “Marry Me,” “Church,” “Love of My Life,” and “Nobody’s Watching.” There’s also a Target-exclusive bonus track titled “After Love (Part 2).”

“We had the script, and then we picked places where we would like to put the music,” Lopez told Billboard about the soundtrack process. “You can’t have a movie about two pop stars who are performing and not have a soundtrack.”

Lopez said she listened to 100 songs from different producers and songwriters before narrowing it down. You can pre-save the soundtrack before its release here.
