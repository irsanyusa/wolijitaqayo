An Artist Creates Show-Stopping Portraits With Just an Iron and Tulle

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Benjamin Shine is an artist who has spent more than 10 years perfecting his unique art of ironing and layering tulle. He decided to work with this fabric because it’s more suitable for creating shading. His portraits can be turned into amazing sculptures for both inside and outside, and he even took his work to the fashion world.
