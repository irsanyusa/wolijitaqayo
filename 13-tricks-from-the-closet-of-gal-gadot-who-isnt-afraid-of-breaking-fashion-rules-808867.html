13 Tricks From the Closet of Gal Gadot, Who Isnt Afraid of Breaking Fashion Rules

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Gal Gadot is not just a popular actress but also a former participant in “Miss Universe.” She’s on the list of the most attractive women in the world. The star of the superhero movie Wonder Woman wears amazing outfits on the red carpets and in movies. But in her everyday life, she prefers comfortable clothes and simple makeup.

We at Bright Side have been charmed by Gal Gadot, a talented actress and mother of 3, so we decided to find out what fashion life hacks we could all learn from her.

You should dress the way you want.

It might seem that Gal Gadot has a perfect fashion sense. But, when she was taking part in “Miss Universe,” she wasn’t very interested in fashion. The actress said that she used to wear terrible clothes at the time. Even though she had a lot of dresses in her suitcase, she wore pants to breakfasts and lunches for the participants.

And today, she might easily forget to remove the tag from a new blouse before going to a restaurant. Or wearing a suit instead of a dress, as she did during the premiere of Ralph Breaks the Internet.

Shoes without heels are not only comfortable, but also elegant.

DDNY / Broadimage / East News, Gilbert Flores / Broadimage / East News, Evan Agostini / Invision / AP / East News

We’re used to seeing actresses in high heels during movie premiers and other events. But Gal once wore flats on the red carpet and then continued to do so.

She shared in an interview, “I love wearing high heels — it’s beautiful, it’s sexy, whatever. But at the same time, especially stilettos, it puts us out of balance. We can fall any minute.”

Deep cleavage

Jordan Strauss / Invision / AP / East News, Evan Agostini / Invision / AP / East News, Evan Agostini / Invision / AP / East News

The actress often chooses long dresses with deep cleavage, revealing the shoulders. Gal doesn’t look provocative because she prefers simple makeup and minimal jewelry.

By the way, internet users have criticized the choice of Gal for the role of Wonder Woman. Gal has smaller breasts than the character from the comics. But she doesn’t care, “On the whole, I just forget all that and think about how lucky I am to be in this position, to be playing such an iconic, important character and on such a wild adventure.”

Loose shirt, shorts, and glasses are a great summer outfit.

The long-legged 37-year-old Gal Gadot can often be seen in mini-skirts, but in her free time, she often wears denim shorts. She also wears a loose shirt and aviator or cat eye sunglasses.

Don’t be afraid of bright colors.

Gilbert Flores / Broadimage / East News, Anthony Behar / Sipa USA / East News

When choosing monochromatic outfits and bright colors, Gal Gadot is not afraid of being overshadowed by her dress or suit. And it’s because she doesn’t use too many accessories or pieces of jewelry, and chooses simple shoes. For example, she once appeared at an event wearing a solid red midi Dior dress with a couple of rings on her ring fingers.

Red lipstick is a great accessory.

Apega / WENN.com / agefotostock / East News, Evan Agostini / Invision / AP / East News

The Hollywood star uses rich tones of lipsticks that create a great contrast with her dark brown eyes and really dark hair. Her preferences are so constant that she used the same red lipstick 2 times in a row: during the Golden Globes and then at the Oscars in 2018.

But her character from Wonder Woman: 1984 wears lipstick in neutral colors, even though in comics, she has bright red lips. The actress explains that, in the film, her character is not supposed to attract too much attention.

She’s not afraid to shine. Literally.

DailyCeleb / Sipa USA / East News, Hahn Lionel / ABACA / East News, AFP / East News

If you’ve always wanted to go to a party wearing a shiny dress, but you’re scared, just look at Gal. This young woman is not afraid of wearing sparkly dresses in front of millions of people. Some of her outfits even have 2 types of shiny fabrics: gold and silver. They look really bold, reminding us of the jazz age, when women wore sparkling dresses.

Stay true to your taste.

face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News, face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

A comfortable solid tracksuit and soft leopard slippers or leggings and a warm sweater — at home, the Hollywood star looks like most ordinary women. She says, “I have my own style, which is completely simple. I like to look a little bit sophisticated.”

Don’t be afraid of horizontal stripes.

There’s an opinion that horizontal stripes make us appear bigger, and vertical stripes make us appear slimmer. Gal Gadot is not someone who believes this. When she was wearing a midi dress with black and white stripes, she proved that she doesn’t look bigger in it.

Besides, if you read the idea of the geometric illusion by Helmholtz, you will see that stripes not just don’t make you look bigger, but they may actually make you look fit.

A beige coat goes well with anything.

Beige coats can be worn with anything, because beige is a neutral color and it can be used as a base for any outfit. It doesn’t attract attention and always looks good with jeans and dresses. Gal Gadot has at least 2 beige things: a cropped trench and a long beige coat with black buttons that she looks amazing in.

The best skincare is a healthy diet.

Gal knows that it’s not about how much cream you use, but how much water you drink during the day, in order to stay hydrated. She also says that she practices the Mediterranean diet. She adds, “I eat a lot of fish and huge salads with olive oil and lemon.” She doesn’t forget to moisturize her skin and uses as little makeup as possible when she is not at work.

Drying hair without a hairdryer is a simple solution.

Gal is very protective of her naturally dark hair. And her secret is simple, “People tell me not to wash my hair every day. I wash my hair every day, but I let it dry naturally!”

Due to the intense air motion from a hairdryer, hair loses all of its moisture quickly, which leads to damage. Studies show: the hotter the air, the more cracks there are in the hair cuticle. So, even if you are using a hairdryer, let your hair rest from time to time, like Gal Gadot does.

1980s clothes look great in the 2020s.

In Wonder Woman 1984, Gal’s character Diana wears stylish clothes that were very trendy in the ’80s. For example, in one of the scenes, she appears in a loose shirt, a vest, and a gray jacket with wide shoulders and rolled sleeves.

These jackets look masculine, because in the ’70s and ’80s, women started to build careers in the areas that used to be “for men.” And jackets were a symbol of the changing times. Today, now that the ’80s are popular again, Gal uses the retro look of her character in real life.
