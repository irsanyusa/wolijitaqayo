Music star is not pregnant

Publish date: 2024-09-13

But her manager and old friend, Benny Medina has put an end to the rumours on Thursday, August 11, 2016. He told TMz the stories about Jennifer being pregnant is false and "100% not true."

She also backed up her statement with some visual evidence in the form of a picture taken of the singer after her show in Vegas on Wednesday, August 10, 2016. In the photo, JLo can be seen in a tangerine bandeau top and matching straight cut trousers.

"Does this look like pregnant?" Medina told TMZ.
