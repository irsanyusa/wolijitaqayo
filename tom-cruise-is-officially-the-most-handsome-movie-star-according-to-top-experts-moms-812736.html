Tom Cruise Is Officially the Most Handsome Movie Star According to Top Experts, Moms

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Being a mom — or a dad — means you’re of a certain age and usually stay true to whatever preferences you have. This explains why in a survey given by the film company Showcase Cinemas, 2,000 mothers from all over the world voted Tom Cruise “the most attractive actor of all time” in 2022.

It seems that the sequel to Top Gun, Top Gun: Maverick, has brought the 60-year-old star’s masculine charm back to the forefront. Cruise has, once again, become the ultimate heartthrob, just as he was in his earlier films, like the original Top Gun in 1986 and Cocktail in 1988, where he played the neighborhood bartender that everyone fell in love with.

Invision/Invision/East News

Tom Cruise, who is 60 years old, received the highest number of votes in the poll, while The Sound of Music, starring Julie Andrews and released in 1965, was voted as the most beloved film by the participating mothers. However, the representative of the poll organization commented on the results, stating that although The Sound of Music came out as number one, the competition was intense as numerous classic films made it into the top 10.
