Publish date: 2024-07-03

Selena Gomez proved once again that she is the Queen of body positivity. Her recent post showing her soaking up the sun in an eye-catching swimsuit has certainly excited everyone.
And Selena also shared a jaw-dropping secret about her Instagram page that made us admire her honesty.

She’s glowing.

Gomez who has an impressive number of 424 million Instagram followers has shared a gorgeous photo from her recent summer vacation. The snap, which was featured in her stories, was taken on a fancy boat where the singer struck a confident pose in her beautiful black swimsuit.

The photo perfectly captured the spirit of summer as the star sat on a grey towel, positioning her hands behind her head. Her gaze was fixated on the blue skies and the radiant beams of sunlight.

A fun fact about her Instagram page.

Despite being one of the most followed celebrities on Instagram and regularly posting on the platform, Selena recently got totally honest about her presence on the social platform.

Gomez admitted that she delegates the management of her Instagram account to an assistant, as she doesn’t personally oversee it entirely. She noted, ’’I created a system. Everything I do I send to my assistant who posts them. As far as comments, my team will put together a few things that are encouraging.’’

The Good For You singer explained, ’’The only thing I have on my phone is TikTok because I find it to be a little less hostile.’’

However, she acknowledged all the positive aspects of Instagram, such as ’’connecting with fans, seeing how happy and excited they are and their stories.’’ That said, Gomez admitted that now everything on the platform is ’’filtered through’’ for her.

We love how Selena is always transparent with her fans whether it comes to her professional or personal life. And in the past, the star opened up about her relationship to her body and the changes it is going to, as she admitted that she is embracing her figure whichever size it gets.
